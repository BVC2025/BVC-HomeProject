User shared a Gantt chart for BVC24 AI ERP System Implementation. Treat as **reference only** — not a contract. Use it to sanity-check which phase a piece of work belongs to.

**Timeline**: 20 weeks. Start 26 May 2025. Go-live 6 Oct 2025. AI Integration kickoff 21 Jul 2025.

**Phases**:
1. Project Initiation & Analysis (Weeks 1–2) — kickoff, requirements, process study, gap analysis
2. Planning & Design (Weeks 3–4) — ERP architecture, infra/DB, business process mapping, UI/UX
3. ERP System Development (Weeks 5–10) — Core ERP + HR/Payroll, Inventory & Warehouse, Purchase & Vendor, Sales & CRM, Finance & Accounting, Production & BOM, Quality Management modules
4. AI Integration & Automation (Weeks 9–12) — AI chatbot, analytics dashboard, forecasting, workflow automation
5. Integration & Data Migration (Weeks 13–15) — Third-party APIs/devices, data migration, master data setup
6. Testing & Validation (Weeks 15–18) — System test, UAT, performance/security, bug fixing
7. Deployment & Support (Weeks 18–20) — Employee training, go-live, post-go-live support

How to apply:
- The biometric + AI allocation work cuts across **Phase 4 (AI Integration)** and **Phase 5 (Device Integration)** — that's where the user's current ask sits.
- Existing repo already covers chunks of Phase 3 (HR, Inventory, Vendor, Project/Task modules) — extend, don't rebuild.
